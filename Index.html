Delete Your Oviyas Meraki Account

If you'd like to delete your Oviyas Meraki account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Oviyas Meraki app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

Option 2: Request via Email

Email us at support@oviyasmeraki.com with the subject: Delete My Account. Include the email address or username linked to your account.

We will process your request within 7 working days.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone.
